The Process
How your
investment works

A straightforward, four-stage journey from capital commitment to return on investment.

01
Expression of Interest
We present the project details, financials, and risk profile. You review and ask questions before committing.
02
Legal Agreement
A formal loan agreement is executed, with your capital secured against the project property via registered second mortgage.
03
Development Phase
Crown Family Homes manages construction. You receive regular progress updates and milestone reporting throughout.
04
Return of Capital
Upon project completion and sale, your capital plus agreed returns are repaid in full from settlement proceeds.
Our Principles
Why investors choose
Saville Projects

We believe private investors deserve the same quality of opportunity and transparency typically reserved for institutional capital.

Property-Backed Security

Every investment is secured by a registered second mortgage over real property — a tangible, legal interest in a real asset.

Defined Timelines

Projects run 3–24 months with clear milestones. No open-ended commitments — you know when your capital returns.

Full Transparency

Receive project financials, build updates, and milestone reports throughout. No surprises, no guesswork.

Aligned Interests

We only profit when the project succeeds. Our fees and returns are structured so your success is ours — no conflicts of interest.

Primary Lender Secured

Senior financing is already in place. Private investors fill the equity gap — with clearly defined capital stack positioning from day one.

Simple, Clear Agreements

Plain-language legal agreements. We encourage every investor to seek independent legal and financial advice before committing.
